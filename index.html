About the Studio

A creative-first recording studio built for artists who care about sound, energy, and atmosphere. From raw demos to final masters, we provide a space that inspires performance. Our goal at Group 1 Entertainment Studios is to deliver a fresh and comfortable recording experience for an unbeatable price. We want people to feel welcome in our creative space. Located in the Marietta/Atlanta area, we perform professional recording, mixing, mastering, custom production, ghost writing, and creative development services. We specialize in, but are not limited to, Hip-hop, R&B, Pop, and Alternative music.
